##  Overview
This repository contains an **interactive Car Sales Dashboard** built using **Power BI**.  
The dashboard provides insights into **Year-to-Date (YTD)** and **Month-to-Date (MTD)** sales performance, helping stakeholders analyze trends, pricing behavior, company performance, and regional sales distribution.

---

##  Objectives
- Track overall car sales performance and growth
- Compare YTD vs MTD metrics
- Identify top-performing companies, regions, and body styles
- Analyze weekly sales trends and average pricing
- Support data-driven business decisions

---

##  Key KPIs
- YTD Total Sales
- MTD Total Sales
- YTD Average Price
- MTD Average Price
- YTD Cars Sold
- MTD Cars Sold
- Percentage change vs previous period
